World life expectancy

World life expectancy is a critical metric reflecting the average number of years an individual can expect to live, and it serves as an essential indicator of a population's overall health and well-being. As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, the global average life expectancy was approximately 72-73 years. This statistic is not uniform worldwide, exhibiting significant regional disparities influenced by various factors.
